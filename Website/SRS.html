SpendSense is a personal budgeting app geared towards young adults navigating new financial responsibilities in college. It tracks each user's balance, spending trends, and progress towards any chosen goals. Our intuitive interface will allow anyone on the app to view their finances at a glance, which is perfect for full-time students on the go.
